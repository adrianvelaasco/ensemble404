Isay R.G / IY / ((I/O))’s interest explores the intersection of sound craftsmanship, social narrative, and scientific inquiry through three distinct creative identities. 

Isay R.G focuses on musical composition for stage and cinema, alongside popular music production; IY develops visceral multimedia narratives merging film, performance, and sound spatialization to interrogate social politics, migration, and the complexities of the psyche and mental health; and ((I/O)) investigates the structural parallels between neuroscience and the cosmos, employing sensors and data-driven tools to translate scientific phenomena into immersive installations.

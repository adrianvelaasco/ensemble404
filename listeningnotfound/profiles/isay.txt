Isay R.G / IY / ((I/O))’s interest explores the intersection of sound craftsmanship, social narrative, and scientific inquiry through three distinct creative identities. 

Isay R.G focuses on musical composition for stage and cinema; IY develops visceral multimedia narratives to interrogate social politics, migration, and the complexities of the psyche and mental health; ((I/O)) investigates the structural parallels between neuroscience and the cosmos, employing sensors and data-driven tools.

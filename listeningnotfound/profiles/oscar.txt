Is an Italian composer based in Hamburg.  Now he's a PhD candidate at the Hochschule für Musik und Theater under Alexander Schubert and Georg Hajdu, focusing on Al and collective improvisation with Ensemble 404. 

His compositional work spans electronic, instrumental, vocal, improvisation and music theatre. He has collaborated with Alexander Schubert, Berliner Philharmoniker, La Biennale di Venezia, Lux Nova Duo, and the Elbphilharmonie, among others.

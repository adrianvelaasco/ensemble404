Is a composer and multimedia artist based in Hamburg. His work is characterized by a fragmented and maximalist language that investigates the dialogue between contemporary and pop/urban aesthetics. He addresses issues related to Internet culture, multiple identities and privacy in the digital age.

His works have been performed at renowned venues and festivals such as the Auditorio Nacional de Música, Museo Reina Sofía and the Klub Katarakt Festival at Kampnagel (Hamburg).

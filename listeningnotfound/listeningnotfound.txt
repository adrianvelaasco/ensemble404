Listening Not Found is an immersive and performative multimedia installation that transforms the concert into a living, navigable experience. Visitors move freely through a dynamic sonic landscape of live performers, spatial audio, and AI-generated “ghost instruments.” Blurring reality and illusion, presence and absence, the work invites active listening as a personal, embodied and intentional act. Each journey is unique: an exploration of perception, authenticity, and the fragile boundary between human and machine in our post-digital world.
